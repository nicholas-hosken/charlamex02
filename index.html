About us

Being expats ourselves, we know how intimidating the move to Cancún can be, especially when you can't even speak the language. A simple trip to the supermarket can seem quite daunting; and a visit down to the INM, to begin the process of obtaining official ID documents, downright terrifying.

With Charlamex we wish to help our clients not only overcome the language barrier but also become more familiar with the local culture. We do this by bringing individuals and small groups of expats together with qualified, local Spanish tutors. Our tutors are a healthy blend of native and non-native speakers who are all fully committed teaching professionals.

Your first class will begin with us working on the foundational Spanish everyone needs in their day to day life here in Mexico. As your skills progress and your confidence with the basics improves, we can then offer a more bespoke class where we focus on your own personal goals such as communicating with work colleagues, prospective clients or even your in-laws!

All our tutors also have an excellent level of English and so are fully equipped to understand and help you reach these goals. We also have a proven track record which shows how our services have saved others like you months, if not years, of unnecessary insecurity and frustration.
